Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the YourGuide app?

YourGuide is designed to help you find and book tours easily. To get started:

  1. Download and install the app from the App Store
  2. Register using your full name, email address, and password
  3. Browse available tours and their prices on the main page
  4. Select a tour you're interested in
  5. Complete the required information for booking
  6. Complete the payment process

How do I create an account?

Creating a YourGuide account is simple:

  1. Open the YourGuide app
  2. Tap on the Registration option
  3. Enter your full name, email address, and create a password
  4. Submit your registration

All personal information is securely stored according to our privacy policy.

How do I log in to my account?

To log in to your YourGuide account:

  1. Open the YourGuide app
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Tap the login button

If you've forgotten your password, please contact our support team.

How do I book a tour?

Booking a tour with YourGuide is easy:

  1. Browse the available tours on the main page
  2. Check the tour details and price
  3. Select the tour you want to book
  4. Fill in the required booking information
  5. Complete the payment process
  6. You'll receive a confirmation of your booking

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team in several ways:

  • Go to the profile section in the app
  • Tap on any of the contact icons (Instagram, Facebook, Email, or WhatsApp)
  • Send us a message with your question or issue
  • Alternatively, you can directly contact us via:
    • Email: info@yourguide.az
    • WhatsApp: +994 55 686 8509
